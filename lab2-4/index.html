Children's Lessons

Children's full day lessons meet from 10 am through 3:30 pm and include lift access, retals (ski or snowboard), and lunch. All children (age 4-12) are welcome regardless of ability or prior experience. Our expit ski and snowboard instructors provide special care and attention to teach children the correct way to ski and ride, from the basics of carving turns to safety instruction to advanced lessons and multi- week programs. We focus on having fun in a safe and responsible way!

Terrain Park Lessons

Our terrain park lessons meet from 12:30 pm to 4pm at the base of the Powder Keg Terrain Park and include lift access, rentals (ski or snowboard), and an afternoon snack. We start with the bassics and process through all skill levels. Learn how to ride the tails, half pipe, and jumps. Must be 13 years old. Our proven techniques will teach you how to maneuver through any terrian park in a fun and safy way!

Adult Lessons

Adult full-day lessons from 9am through 3pm include lift access, retals(ski or snowboard),and lunch. Adults are divided by ability and experience. We have expect instructors for all levels!
